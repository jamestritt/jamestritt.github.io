Advanced Technology Manufacturing

We have decades of experience in semiconductor facility design and construction. We have managed facility engineering teams for the operation and maintenance of 24 x 7 semiconductor manufacturing facilities for a Fortune 50 company. Our experience also includes battery manufacturing, data centers, and energy centers.

Services we offer specific to Advanced Technology manufacturing industries include;

  • Owners representation / staff augmentation
  • Scope of Work preparation
  • CFD modeling for building envelope and data centers
  • Change management training
  • Piping and Instrumentation Diagram (P&ID) Review
  • Codes & Standards compliance (including hazardous occupancy codes)
  • Design Review
  • Detailed stress analysis
  • Fabrication drawing review
  • Root Cause Corrective Action
  • Weld Procedure Qualifications & Specification
  • Constructive confrontation training
