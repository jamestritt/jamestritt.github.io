Modeling & Simulation

We have decades of experience in Modeling & Simulation. This includes the development of a mathematical abstraction that allows one to examine interactions easily, extends back to archimedes and approximation to reality. The models that we use are analtyic, computational and statistical—Design of experiments (DOE).

Services we offer specific to Modeling & Simulation industries include;

  • Finite element modeling of 2D and 3D structures
  • Advanced material models
  • Computation fluid dynamics
  • Multiphysics analysis

PAST PROJECTS

Several of our engineers have been involved in these types of projects:

  • Fatigue analysis of drill string in the oil & petrochemical industry
  • Finite element analysis of welding distortion for multi-pass welds
  • Weld distortion analysis of a flange on a wind tower
  • Noise attenuation design management for semiconductor Fab manufacturing
  • Energy savings conceptualization and implementation for Fab manufacturing
