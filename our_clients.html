OUR CLIENTS

Who we work for

We have a wide variety of corporate clients, ranging in size from multi-national Fortune 500 companies through pre-revenue startup ventures. We attract clients that are developing new technologies, new products, or in need of engineering analysis of existing systems. Frequently, our clients are looking for novel ‘outside the box’ thinking and concepts that produce 10X results. We partner with clients through a full range of offered services. Some have temporary needs beyond their existing staff, while others have longer term interest in a capability that we possess and seek education and technology transfer. A number of clients use our technical management skills to ensure smooth program completion. We can supply key staff members to interact seamlessly within your own engineering organization, or alternately, we can act as a stand-alone service department that brings your concept into fruition.
