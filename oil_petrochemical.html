Oil & Petrochemical

We have decades of experience in refinery design and associated pressure equipment including hydrocrackers, fluidized catalytic crackers, heat exchangers, tank design, and piping design. Whether you need to determine compliance with various codes and standards or have encountered situations that require detailed stress analysis, we can help you. In addition, we have experts in reliability and fitness for service (API-579). Services we offer specific to the oil and petrochemical industries are as follows:

  • Process, and Instrument Diagram (P&ID) Review
  • Codes & Standards Compliance
  • Fitness for Service
  • Detailed Stress Analysis
  • Fabrication Drawing Review
  • Design Services
  • Specification Writing

PAST PROJECTS

Several of our engineers have been involved in these types of projects:

  • Development of Ultrasonic Weld Parameters for Sheet Structure
  • Design/Layout of Oil Treating Facilities Through Final Construction
  • Design of Hydrocrackers to ASME Section VIII, Division 2
  • Specification of Acceptable Support Spacing for Off-Plot Piping
  • NOx Reduction Facilities Design
  • Specification and Design Standards for Feed/Effluent High Pressure Heat Exchanges Under “Burst Tube” Loading Scenarios
